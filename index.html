About Us

The Sacred Art of Sutika Paricharya

In Ayurveda, the 40 days after childbirth — known as "Sutika Kala" — is the most sacred period for a mother's complete physical and spiritual rejuvenation.

This ancient wisdom focuses on restoring the mother's Agni (digestive fire), balancing the Vata dosha, and promoting holistic healing through warm oil massages, nourishing Satvik foods, herbal remedies, and spiritual practices.
